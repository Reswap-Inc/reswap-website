FitShare is where USC students trade, sell, and discover outfits for every moment—game days, formals, themed parties, internships, and more.

What is FitShare?

FitShare is your campus closet on demand. Instead of buying brand new, explore pre‑loved pieces from students who’ve already rocked them and give your old fits a second life.

How it Works

If you are a Lendor:

  • Browse, pick an item, message vendor, pick number of days you want to borrow, accepted/ rejected, send deposit money.
  • Meet up with vendor or lockers, take picture of item, use, take a picture before returning it.
  • Return, get your calculated amount back based on how long you used it.

If you are a Seller:

  • Browse, pick an item, message vendor, pick number of days you want to borrow, accepted/ rejected, send deposit money.
  • Meet up with vendor or lockers, take picture of item, use, take a picture before returning it.
  • Return, get your calculated amount back based on how long you used it.
