How FitShare Works

As a Member:

  • Browse fitness equipment and gear shared by community members in your area.
  • Message the owner, agree on rental terms, and make secure payment through the app.
  • Meet up safely, document item condition, enjoy your workout, and return when done.
  • Rate your experience and build trust within the FitShare community.

As a Sharer:

  • List your unused fitness equipment and set your own rental rates and availability.
  • Connect with fellow fitness enthusiasts who need the gear you're not using.
  • Arrange safe handovers, earn money from idle equipment, and help others reach their goals.
  • Build your reputation as a trusted community member and expand your network.
