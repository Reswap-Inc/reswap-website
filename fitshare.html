How FitShare Works

Lendor

Share your fitness equipment and fashion items with fellow students

  • List your items with photos and descriptions
  • Set your availability and pickup locations
  • Connect with borrowers in your campus community
  • Earn money while helping other students stay fit and stylish

Borrower

Access fitness equipment and fashion items when you need them

  • Browse available items in your area
  • Message owners to arrange pickup details
  • Use items for your workouts or events
  • Return items safely and rate your experience
