Welcome to

FitShare

FitShare is where USC students trade, sell, and discover outfits for every moment—game days, formals, themed parties, internships, and more.

What is FitShare?

FitShare is your campus closet on demand. Instead of buying brand new, explore pre‑loved pieces from students who've already rocked them and give your old fits a second life.

Save Money & Space

Refresh your look without blowing your budget.

Sustainable Style

Reduce fashion waste one swap at a time.

Student-to-student Trust

Every fit comes from your own campus community.

How FitShare Works

Lender

Share your fitness equipment and fashion items with fellow students

  • List your items with photos and descriptions
  • Set your availability and pickup locations
  • Connect with borrowers in your campus community
  • Earn money while helping other students stay fit and stylish

Borrower

Access fitness equipment and fashion items when you need them

  • Browse available items in your area
  • Message owners to arrange pickup details
  • Use items for your workouts or events
  • Return items safely and rate your experience

Why FitShare Matters

FitShare makes fitness more accessible and sustainable. Every piece of equipment you share is one less item sitting unused and one more person empowered to achieve their health goals without breaking the bank.
