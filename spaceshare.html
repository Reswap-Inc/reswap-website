SpaceShare centralizes Off-Campus student housing into one trusted, campus-first platform.

What is SpaceShare?

SpaceShare is ReSwap's student housing platform designed to fix the broken discovery process. Students can list subleases for specific timeframes, join an existing lease, or browse full housing listings. Real, student-verified housing near campus.

Affordable Flexibility

Save money with semester-friendly lease options designed around how students live.

Student-Verified

Every listing comes from real students or trusted housing partners in your campus community.

Stress-Free Move

Find or list a space fast without the back-and-forth or the paperwork headache.

How SpaceShare Works

Connecting students who need space with those who have it, and partnering with housing providers to make subleasing seamless.
