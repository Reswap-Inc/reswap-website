NeedShare is where USC students pass along the essentials — textbooks, dorm gear, kitchen tools, and more so nothing goes to waste and you save big.

What is NeedShare?

NeedShare is your campus exchange for gently used must‑haves. Instead of buying new, grab what another student is done with. Cheaper for you, better for the planet.

How it Works

If you are a Borrower:

  • 1

    Browse, pick an item, message vendor, pick number of days you want to borrow, accepted/ rejected, send deposit money.

  • 2

    Meet up with vendor or lockers, take picture of item, use, take a picture before returning it.

  • 3

    Return, get your calculated amount back based on how long you used it.

If you are a Buyer:

  • Browse, pick an item, message vendor, pick number of days you want to borrow, accepted/ rejected, send deposit money.

  • Meet up with vendor or lockers, take picture of item, use, take a picture before returning it.

  • Return, get your calculated amount back based on how long you used it.
