What is NeedShare?

NeedShare is your campus essential sharing platform. Instead of buying everything new, connect with fellow students to share items you need temporarily and help others in return.

Local & Trusted

Only verified students can list, share, and borrow items safely.

Easy Sharing

List items in seconds and connect with students who need them.

Save Money

Access what you need without the hefty price tag of buying new.

How NeedShare Works

If you are a Borrower:

1

Browse, pick an item, message vendor, pick number of days you want to borrow, accept/reject, send deposit money

2

Meet up with vendor or lockers, take picture of item use, take a picture before returning it

3

Return, get your calculated amount back based on how long you used it

If you are a Buyer:

1

Browse, pick an item, message vendor, pick number of days you want to borrow, accept/reject, send deposit money

2

Meet up with vendor or lockers, take picture of item use, take a picture before returning it

3

Return, get your calculated amount back based on how long you used it
