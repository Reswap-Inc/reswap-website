Welcome to

TripShare

TripShare helps students heading to the same place connect and share rides. Whether it's an airport run, a weekend trip, or a drive home, split the cost and go together instead of paying full price alone.

What is TripShare?

TripShare is your campus ride-sharing community. Instead of covering gas or ride costs on your own, join other students traveling the same direction and split the cost.
More affordable travel. Real student connections. Built for campus life.

Save Money on Every Trip

Split gas and travel costs with students already heading your way.

Community-Powered Rides

Connect with other students and travel together.

Student-to-Student Trust

Every ride is created by a verified student. Riders are accepted by the ride owner.

How TripShare Works

Ride Creator

Share your ride and lower your travel costs.

  • Create a ride and set your destination
  • List how many seats are available
  • Review and accept riders who request to join
  • Split the total cost with everyone in the car

Joining a Ride

Get where you're going without paying full price.

  • Browse rides going to your destination
  • Request to join available trips
  • Join the ride once accepted by the driver
  • Split the cost and travel together
