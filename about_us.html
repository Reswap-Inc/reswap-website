About ReSwap

Built by USC students, for USC students. ReSwap makes college life simpler, cheaper, and more sustainable by connecting students through a trusted campus marketplace.

Our Story

From Dorm Room Idea to Campus Revolution

ReSwap started when three USC students got tired of seeing perfectly good items thrown away at the end of each semester. We realized that what one student no longer needed could be exactly what another student was looking for.

What began as a simple idea to reduce waste and save money has grown into a thriving campus community that's changing how students think about consumption, sustainability, and supporting each other.

Our Mission

Building Community

Creating meaningful connections between students through shared resources and mutual support.

Our Values

Trust & Safety

Every user is verified through their USC email, creating a trusted environment where students can transact with confidence.

Students First

Every decision we make prioritizes the student experience, from zero transaction fees to features designed specifically for campus life.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform based on student feedback, always looking for new ways to serve our community better.
