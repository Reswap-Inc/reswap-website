How NeedShare Works

Lendor

Share your essential items with fellow students

  • List textbooks, electronics, and household items
  • Set rental periods and pickup locations
  • Help other students access what they need
  • Earn money from items you're not using

Borrower

Access essential items without the full purchase cost

  • Browse available essentials in your campus area
  • Contact owners to arrange borrowing details
  • Use items for studying, cooking, or daily needs
  • Return items on time and leave feedback
