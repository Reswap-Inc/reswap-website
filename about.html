Who We Are

ReSwap is a student-powered platform that makes college life easier. Whether you’re looking for housing, selling your old items, or trying to find a ride, this is where USC students go to figure it out together.

Brand Mission

To empower students through a trusted, community-first marketplace that simplifies college living, reduces waste, and helps them focus on what really matters - school and growth.

Brand Vision

Four student-powered communities, each built to make college life simpler. Discover where you belong.
